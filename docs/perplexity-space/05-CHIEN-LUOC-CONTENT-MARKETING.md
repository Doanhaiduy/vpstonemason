## 2. CÁC TRỤ CỘT NỘI DUNG (Content Pillars)

### Pillar 1: 🪨 Product Education (Giáo dục sản phẩm) — 30%
- So sánh các loại đá
- Hướng dẫn chọn đá
- Giới thiệu sản phẩm mới
- Thickness, finish, edge profiles explained
- **Ví dụ**: "Granite vs Marble: Which is Best for Your Kitchen?"

### Pillar 2: 🏠 Inspiration & Projects (Cảm hứng & Dự án) — 25%
- Before/After transformations
- Completed project showcases
- Design trends
- Colour & material pairing ideas
- **Ví dụ**: "Stunning Calacatta Gold Kitchen — Brighton Project Reveal"

### Pillar 3: 💡 Tips & How-To (Mẹo & Hướng dẫn) — 20%
- Stone care & maintenance
- Kitchen renovation planning
- Budget tips
- DIY vs Professional advice
- **Ví dụ**: "5 Mistakes to Avoid When Choosing Your Kitchen Benchtop"

### Pillar 4: 🏢 Behind The Scenes (Hậu trường) — 15%
- Workshop/fabrication videos
- Team spotlights
- Day-in-the-life content
- Process videos (từ slab → benchtop)
- **Ví dụ**: "Watch our CNC machine cut a perfect Calacatta slab"

### Pillar 5: ⭐ Social Proof (Bằng chứng xã hội) — 10%
- Customer testimonials
- Google reviews highlight
- Client stories
- Awards/certifications
- **Ví dụ**: "James & Sarah share their kitchen renovation journey"

## 7. VIDEO CONTENT STRATEGY

### YouTube / Facebook Video Ideas:
1. **"From Slab to Kitchen" series** — full fabrication process (3-5 min)
2. **"Stone School" series** — educational videos about each stone type (2-3 min)
3. **"Project Reveal" series** — completed project walkthroughs (2-4 min)
4. **"Ask the Stonemason"** — Q&A answering common questions (1-2 min)
5. **"The Making Of"** — behind-the-scenes at the workshop (3-5 min)

### Video Production Tips:
- Film horizontal (16:9) for YouTube/Facebook, vertical (9:16) for Reels/Stories
- Good lighting — especially for stone colour accuracy
- Show close-ups of stone texture and veining
- Include text overlays for key information
- Add music (royalty-free) for atmosphere
- Include your logo watermark
- Always end with CTA: website, phone, showroom address

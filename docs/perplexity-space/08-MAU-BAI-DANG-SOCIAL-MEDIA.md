### Template 3.3 — CSF Education:
```
⚠️ IMPORTANT: THE ENGINEERED STONE BAN IN AUSTRALIA

Did you know? Since July 1, 2024, engineered stone with high crystalline silica content is BANNED in Australia.

Why? To protect stone workers from silicosis — a serious lung disease caused by inhaling silica dust.

What does this mean for you?
✅ Natural stones (granite, marble, quartzite) are NOT affected
✅ Porcelain/sintered surfaces are fully compliant
✅ New CSF (Crystalline Silica Free) engineered stone is available
❌ Traditional quartz benchtops can no longer be sold

At PVstoneau, ALL our products are 100% compliant 🛡️

Have questions? We're here to help!
```
